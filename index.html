Little Charms Project

Empowering Children with Little Charms of Hope

The Little Charms Project (widely recognized as Little Charms or Little Charm) is a dedicated youth-led initiative providing monthly mental and physical health education to children. Through interactive programs in schools and libraries, we gift handmade charm bracelets that serve as symbolic "Little Charms" of strength and resilience for every child we meet.
